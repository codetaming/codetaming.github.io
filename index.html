It is easy to lose control of a codebase over the years. Let us help make working heritage applications productive again, reducing maintenance costs and speeding up future development. Talk to us: hello@codetaming.org.

Consulting

Has your codebase become a headache. Do you want to start getting to grips with it?

We can perform a thorough code audit, talk with you about what you want to achieve and put together a step-by-step plan to help you get back on track.

We recommend tools, training and practical steps to achieve reliable, maintainable code that you can confidently build upon.

Training

We offer custom courses tailored to the needs of your development team and project. Topics include:

  • Test-driven Development
  • Refactoring
  • Continuous Integration
  • API Design
  • Use of Design Patterns
  • Source Control Management
  • Effective Documentation

Development

Want us to do it for you? We prefer to give your own team the skills and knowledge to tame, improve and maintain their own code.

However, if you prefer us to get more hands-on either as part of your team or independently that is great too.

We offer a range of different options.
